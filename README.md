
# Tableau Sales & Customer Dashboard

## Overview
This Tableau project provides an interactive analysis of **Sales Performance** and **Customer Insights** for the year 2023. The dashboards help track key business metrics, identify profitable customers, analyze sales trends, and understand customer purchasing behavior.

## Dashboards

### 1. Sales Dashboard
<img width="1207" height="802" alt="Sales Dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a416ca3b-950c-435a-a924-847e1befa271" />

Key insights include:
- Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity
- Year-over-Year (YoY) performance comparison
- Sales and Profit by Product Sub-Category
- Sales and Profit Trends over Time
- Highest and Lowest Performing Months

### 2. Customer Dashboard
<img width="1198" height="802" alt="customer_Dashboard" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5d5e834c-27c6-43ea-aa07-03fa8eb6bac3" />

Key insights include:
- Total Customers
- Sales per Customer
- Total Orders
- Customer Distribution by Number of Orders
- Top 10 Customers by Profit
- Customer Performance Trends

## Features
- Interactive filters (Year, Category, Sub-Category, Region, State, City)
- KPI tracking with YoY comparison
- Trend analysis and performance monitoring
- Customer segmentation and profitability analysis
- Easy-to-use business intelligence dashboard

## Tools Used
- **Tableau Desktop**
- Data Visualization
- Calculated Fields
- Dashboard Actions & Filters

## Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Monitor sales performance
- Identify top customers and profitable products
- Analyze purchasing patterns
- Support data-driven business decisions
